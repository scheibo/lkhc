Team Rider Scoring

This page shows how riders contributed to their team's overall score. For each week, the team score is shown (top line in table) along with a list of riders on the team for that week. For each week for each rider, scores which contributed to the team's score toward the overall for that week are shaded. A green shade means the rider scored by riding the climb, while a blue shade means the rider scored by volunteering or earning a ride credit (for example by attending an event Low-Key wishes to support) and did not ride. For each week the team score shown is the sum of the shaded rider scores. The team overall score (on the right of the first row) is the sum of non-discarded scores for the team. Discarded scores are a light text shade and struck out, while counted scores are a dark text shade. Details of the colors are shown at the bottom of each team's table.
