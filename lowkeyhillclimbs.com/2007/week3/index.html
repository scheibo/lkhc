Overview

The call across the land...

From afar, it calls your name. No voice, no sound. But you hear it all the same.

Today it's the usual route: under the trees, over the hills. But your mind is detached, the present little more than a peripheral destraction. You listen to the call. To Saratoga. To Bohlman.

It knows you. It knows where you are. And it is calling. Calling. Calling.... You must answer. It is the only way. The only way to liberate yourself. The only path to freedom.

Hard Heads

Our insurance demands it: helmets are required. Sorry kids, no exceptions!

Time and Place

The start this week will be in Saratoga, at the intersection of Highway 9 and 6th St. Please don't park on either 6th Street or on Bohlman Road.

Riding to the start is recommended, but if you drive, there's plenty of parking away from the staging area!
