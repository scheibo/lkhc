1998 Low-Key Hillclimbs
Week 2 Results: Hwy 84 - Haskins Hill
10 Oct 1998

Go back to main page.

The Action

Due to construction on the originally planned climb of Highway 84, Low-Key tried something new this week. After starting in San Gregorio and riding the generally flat Highway 84 to Pescadero Road, a right hand turn led to the climb of Haskins Hill. Week 1 winner David Wyandt returned this week to break the half-hour, the only rider to do so, with Mike Podgorski and Michael Dunn coming in within a second of each other for second and third. Among the women, it was once again Cornelia Fletcher at the top, with Colleen Farrell and Meg Gehred second and third.

Thanks to coordinator Kevin Winterfield, with help from Tracy Colwell, Jim Gellman, Robert Murillo, and Some other cool person for making today's climb possible!
