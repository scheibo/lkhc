The Story

After week 7, Eric Albrecht (team: JAH LOVE/Menlo Velo) leads the men's standings, 10 points ahead of 2nd-place Curt Ferguson, with 3rd-place Bryan Buck a full 37.8 points back. Among the women, Cornelia Fletcher (team: JAH LOVE/Menlo Velo) leads by 46.7 points over second-place Phyllis Olrich, with Nancy Beer in third. JAH LOVE/Menlo Velo is at the head of the team standings, followed by TNT and Bikeaholics. JAH LOVE/Menlo Velo's win is by a mind-blowing margin of 2.50 points.
