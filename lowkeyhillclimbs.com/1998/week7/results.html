1998 Low-Key Hillclimbs
Week 7 Results: Mt Hamilton
14 Nov 1998

Go back to main page.

The Action

Due to a motorcycle accident blocking the course, the climb had to be paused for approximately twenty minutes. Riders were restarted in the order and with the gaps with which they arrived. Posted times represent the sum of the first and second split times.

And when the dust cleared, it was Eric Albrecht who took the top position among the men with a solid time of under 1:16. His teammate Cornelia Fletcher held off Laura Stern to repeat her week 1 victory.

Thanks to coordinator Kevin Winterfield, with help from Tracy Colwell, Tom Gardin, Jon Geary, and Randy Nelson for making today's climb possible!
