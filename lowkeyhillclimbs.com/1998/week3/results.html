1998 Low-Key Hillclimbs
Week 3 Results: Soda Springs
17 Oct 1998

Go back to main page.

The Action

Another beautiful day for climbing! And we were honored to have professional bike racer Mat Anand and the Alto Velo category 1 team on hand to give pre-during-post climb advice!

In the men's cateogry, it was Eric Albrecht on top. In the women's standings, Laura Stern came out ahead of weeks 1-2 winner Cornylia Fletcher.

Thanks to coordinator Todd Studenicka, with help from Julia Ames, Roy Ames, Tracy Colwell, Albert Li, Bob McDermand, Daryl Spano, Laura Spano, Julie Studenicka, and Kevin Winterfield for making today's climb possible!
