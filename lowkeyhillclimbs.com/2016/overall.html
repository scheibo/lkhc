Report

Amd this is a WRAP for Low-Key Hillclimbs 2016! Huge thanks to our coordinators, our volunteers, and our riders who all helped make Low-Key 2016 a huge success. This year was different in several ways than Low-Keys past, maybe a bit "low-keyer" in a way, but we hope everyone was able to experience some new climbs, challenge themselves with the inspiration of friendly competition, and to make some new friends. And along the way we raised some good money for the Open Space Trust and Bike Hut.

And special congratulations to our winners! There's no faking your way to the top of the Low-Key standings: it requires superb fitness, dedication, and versatility. So kudos to Katie Quinn, David Collet, and Easy Riders for your success. And congratulations as well to Bill Bushnell and Adam Tow!

And let's not forget the elite 100% club this year: David Collet, Frank Paysen, and Adem Rudin. Given that all three of the GPS climbs were on rainy days, including outright mud on Butano, this category has special meaning in 2016.

But these are just the leaders. Indeed everyone who finished a climb deserves congratulations, because you challenged yourself and accomplished a goal. It's for you that the series exists.
