Mt Hamilton

Week 9x of the 2016 Low-Key Hillclimb Series

Chris Evans sprints to the summit (Alexander Komlik)

Thanks to help from Bill Bushnell and Alexander Komlik for making today's climb possible!

Photos

  • Alexander Komlik
  • Bill Bushnell (photos and blog)

Report

Hamilton isn't quite what it's been in Low-Key past, but riders turned out anyway to mark their times before holiday fun. Results are still in process, so check back later for more.

Today's climb featured Low-Key veteran Eric Wohlberg outpacing oh-so-close 2016 2nd overall Chris Evans to be first to the summit. Eric, however, doesn't believe in riding in a metrology-rich environment, and so Chris is the winner of the Low-Key men's competition for the week. Paul McKenzie rode with Sarah Schroer were the only tandem, winning that division. Bill Bushnell rode a uncharacteristically human pace to win the E-Bike division, as he stopped along the way to take photos.

Nina Komlik did the entire climb to where Alexander was taking photos, but then turned back, within sight of the finish. But you can see her time to the turn-off in the split times.
