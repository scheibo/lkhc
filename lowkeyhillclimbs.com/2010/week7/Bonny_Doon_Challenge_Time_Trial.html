Bonny Doon Time Trial

portion of Stage 2 Challenge ride - 24 January 2009

go back to Low-Key Hillclimb page

Report

Ben Jacques-Maynes of the Bissel Professional Cycling team, 2008 Low-Key Hillclimb champion Tim Clark, and former national level US rider and present-day real estate agent Katrin Tobin pre-rode the climb to establish target times. Nobody without electric motor assistance was able to match either Ben's, Tim's, or Katrin's times on race day, although Steve Heaton and Julie Jag came closest.
