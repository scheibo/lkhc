The Story

After week 9, Tim Clark (team: Red Octane) leads the men's standings, 7.6 points ahead of 2nd-place Clark Foy, with 3rd-place Greg McQuaid a full 40.7 points back. Among the women, Lucia Mokres (team: LGBRC) leads by 13.4 points over second-place Janet Martinez, with Jennie Phillips in third. San Jose Bike Club is at the head of the team standings, followed by Sisters of No Mercy and Bike Trip. San Jose Bike Club's win is by a mind-blowing margin of 64.30 points.
