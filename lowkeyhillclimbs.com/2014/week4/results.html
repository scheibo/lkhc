Berkeley Hills

Week 4 of the 2014 Low-Key Hillclimb Series

The Low-Key crew celebrating having made it to the top of Quarry (Paul McKenzie)
Thanks to coordinator Paul McKenzie for making today's climb possible!

Photos

  • Bernard Demai (South Park finish Facebook)

Report

Paul McKenzie led an enthusiastic group of approximately 20 riders through his excellent Berkeley Hills course today. Despite worries about the rain and wind, we experienced only minor rain, mostly following the Thorndale climb, and the wind wasn't much of a factor until the final descent back to the start. The main issue with the wet were the slippery conditions on the dirt portion of the Quarry climb, but despite some rear-wheel slippage everyone made it to the top without fault.

On points side, this week was led by Bill Laddish in the men and Amy Cameron in the women, with Sisters and Misters of No Mercy by far the dominant team. Sisters and Misters moved to the front of the standings in the Megateam ranking with their impressive 7-rider turn-out, including all three women: Amy, Jennie Phillips, and Trish Pacheco (riding solo). Team CVC, led by Biull Laddish, had 4 riders and was 2nd, while Team Google had 3 including the winning tandem team of Emma and Jonathan Dixon.

Patrick Gordis and Wes Lane joined the group for most of the ride, skipping Quarry and Fish Ranch-Grizzly Peak. Adrian Lobito and Ramon Periquet also did the partial route.
